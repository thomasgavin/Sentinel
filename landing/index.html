Features

Built around people,
not motion.

👤

Household Identity

Every person in your home has a named profile. Family arrivals are silent. Strangers get your attention. This one principle eliminates 80% of false alerts.

"Emma just got home. Front door, 3:47 PM."
📈

Rhythm Learning

After 2 weeks, Sentinel knows your patterns. Michael's usually home by 6:30. When he's not there by 8 PM on a Tuesday, Sentinel notices.

"Michael isn't home yet. It's 8:15. Just flagging."
🚨

Autonomous Response

60-second cancel window. No response? Sentinel contacts your monitoring center, triggers all lights, sounds the exterior alarm, and locks the doors.

"Possible break-in at back door. 60 seconds to cancel or I'm calling it in."
📦

Carrier Recognition

Sentinel recognizes UPS, FedEx, USPS, and Amazon on sight — uniform, vehicle, and behavior. Deliveries are logged silently. Package sitting for 2+ hours while you're away? Then you hear about it.

"That's the Amazon driver — third time this week. Package is on the porch."
🔇

Privacy Mode

One command. "Hey Sentinel, stop watching." Everything pauses. Smoke and glass break detection stay active. Your home, your rules.

"Privacy mode on. I'll stay off until you tell me otherwise."
🔒

Privacy Architecture

The AI chip on the camera extracts a mathematical fingerprint. Only that — never raw video — is sent to the cloud. We never see your family.

"Only a fingerprint leaves your home. Raw video never does."

Household Memory

Gets smarter every week.
Irreplaceable over time.

Sentinel builds a living model of your household — not just faces, but rhythms, relationships, and context. The longer you use it, the more it knows.

Week 1–2

Learning your household

Sentinel enrolls your family, learns your cameras' coverage, and starts building a baseline of who comes and goes.

"I'm still learning your household's routine. I'll flag anything unusual in the meantime."
Week 3–4

Establishing patterns

Confident arrival and departure windows emerge. Recurring visitors get named when you tell Sentinel who they are.

"The motion at the back gate is the neighbor's cat. I've seen it fourteen times. I'm not going to keep logging it."
Month 2+

Fully calibrated

Sentinel proactively flags deviations from established rhythms — absences, unusual vehicles, new recurring visitors.

"Emma didn't come home at her usual time. She left at 8:05 AM and I haven't seen her since. It's 7:30 PM."
🔐

Your data stays yours — and stays valuable

Every week of Sentinel usage deepens your household model: more accurate face recognition, more reliable rhythm patterns, a richer visitor history. This intelligence is specific to your home — no competitor can replicate or transfer it. The product gets more valuable the longer you use it.
